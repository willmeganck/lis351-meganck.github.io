Introduction

Golf has been a central part of my life for as long as I can remember, rooted in the cherished memories of playing with my dad and older brother since childhood. Growing up as a big golfer, those early rounds on the course forged bonds of camaraderie and competition that have shaped me profoundly. Last summer, my passion for the sport led me to work for the Wisconsin State Golf Association, an experience that deepened my appreciation for the intricacies and community surrounding golf. Whether teeing off at dawn or sinking putts under the setting sun, nothing compares to the joy I find in sharing the fairways with friends and family.
