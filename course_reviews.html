Course Reviews

Will's Top 5 WI Course Thoughts

1. Whistling Straits - Sheboygan, WI
Perched along the picturesque shores of Lake Michigan, Whistling Straits mesmerizes golfers with its stunning vistas and challenging links-style layout. Designed by Pete Dye, its rugged terrain, deep bunkers, and windswept dunes demand precision and strategic play. Impeccably maintained fairways and greens elevate the golfing experience, while world-class facilities, including a luxurious clubhouse, enhance the overall appeal.
2. Blackwolf Run - Kohler, WI
Blackwolf Run captivates golfers with its serene setting amidst the Wisconsin countryside. This esteemed course, also designed by Pete Dye, features rolling fairways, meandering rivers, and lush woodlands. Its championship layouts provide a formidable test of skill, while meticulous course maintenance ensures optimal playing conditions. With top-tier amenities and scenic views at every turn, Blackwolf Run offers a memorable golfing experience.
3. Erin Hills - Erin, WI
Nestled in the Kettle Moraine region, Erin Hills enchants golfers with its natural beauty and challenging design. The course, crafted by Michael Hurdzan, Dana Fry, and Ron Whitten, showcases expansive fairways, deep bunkers, and native grasses. Golfers are treated to panoramic views of the rolling landscape, creating an immersive golfing experience. With impeccable course conditions and first-class amenities, Erin Hills remains a premier destination for golf enthusiasts.
4. SentryWorld - Stevens Point, WI
SentryWorld charms golfers with its picturesque landscape and meticulously crafted layout. Designed by Robert Trent Jones Jr., this championship course boasts manicured fairways, pristine greens, and scenic water features. The course's strategic design offers challenges for golfers of all skill levels, while its inviting atmosphere fosters a sense of camaraderie and enjoyment. With modern amenities and warm hospitality, SentryWorld promises a memorable golfing retreat.
5. The Bull at Pinehurst Farms - Sheboygan Falls, WI
The Bull at Pinehurst Farms stands out as a hidden gem amidst the rolling hills of Sheboygan Falls. Designed by Jack Nicklaus, this challenging course features dramatic elevation changes, strategic bunkering, and picturesque views of the surrounding countryside. Golfers are drawn to its championship-caliber layout and pristine course conditions. With exceptional service and a welcoming ambiance, The Bull at Pinehurst Farms offers a memorable golfing experience in Wisconsin.
